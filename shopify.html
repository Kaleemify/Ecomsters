FAQs

Get Answers to Your Ecommerce Automation Questions

Shopify automation refers to the application of tools and technologies that are software-based to improve the efficiency the running of an online shop. By automating tasks like the management of inventory, order processing email marketing, customer interaction, Shopify automation reduces the work required to run your business. This in turn improves operational efficiency, improves customer satisfaction and allows you to grow your business easily.

There are many different things that you can automate using which include:
  • Inventory Management Update stock levels of products in accordance with the sales and data from suppliers.
  • Delivery Fulfillment Automate shipping labels tracker numbers, shipping labels, and notifications to customers.
  • Customer Mails Send automatically orders confirmations and reminders for abandoned carts as well as promotional email.
  • Prices and discounts Set the rules for price updates automatically or promotions based on inventory level or trends in seasonality.
  • Customer Service Chatbots: Use chatbots as well as automated responses to respond to the most common questions of customers.

No, setting up Shopify automation isn't a requirement for technical skills. Our simple-to-use tools and resources allow anyone to automatize the operation of their Shopify store. If you'd require assistance from a professional, Ecomsters offers Shopify automation agency services to assist you in setting up and tweak your shopify automation to get the best outcomes.
